Task instructions

You need to find answers to 10 questions by searching the web and submitting queries to the search interface provided below. Use your best search skills, general knowledge, and quick thinking.

Best MTurk players (who submit most number of correct answers) will receive additional $1 bonus.

There will be 10 questions. Time allotted to answer each questions is 10 minutes. The answer must consist of two parts:

  • The answer text (usually a few words only)
  • One or more links to the supporting pages that should explicitly contain this information.

You should find information using our search interface and browse pages through our proxy (http://ir-ub.mathcs.emory.edu:8100/http/www...) during the search.
Do NOT use your browser "location" bar, search toolbar, or search engine opened in new browser window.

Your browser must support cookies.

An answer is considered correct if there is a web page containing your answer, you provided its URL, and you have find an answer using our search proxy.

An answer is considered INCORRECT if:

  • One of the fields above is missing
  • The provided page does not contain the answer
  • The answer is too vague or does not address the question directly
  • The web page is manipulated by the participant (manually or automatically), to contain the answer.
  • The answer was not found through our search interface (logs will be checked to confirm that our interface was used).
